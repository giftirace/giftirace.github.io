# 雅思C16 T1（2026/3/5)

## 1.parking才是停车位的意思

 **parking 是不可数名词**

在英语里 **parking** 表示“停车位/停车空间”，是**不可数名词**。

所以一般说：

- **plenty of parking** ✔

- **enough parking** ✔

- **limited parking** ✔

  ###### parking lot是停车场

  | 单词        | 中文   | 例句                                         |
  | ----------- | ------ | -------------------------------------------- |
  | information | 信息   | The website provides useful **information**. |
  | advice      | 建议   | She gave me some **advice**.                 |
  | furniture   | 家具   | The room has modern **furniture**.           |
  | equipment   | 设备   | The lab has advanced **equipment**.          |
  | luggage     | 行李   | His **luggage** was lost.                    |
  | baggage     | 行李   | Please collect your **baggage**.             |
  | traffic     | 交通   | There is heavy **traffic** today.            |
  | parking     | 停车位 | There is plenty of **parking** here.         |

| 单词      | 中文 | 例句                                         |
| --------- | ---- | -------------------------------------------- |
| research  | 研究 | She is doing **research** on climate change. |
| work      | 工作 | He has a lot of **work** today.              |
| homework  | 作业 | I finished my **homework**.                  |
| progress  | 进展 | The project made good **progress**.          |
| knowledge | 知识 | He has extensive **knowledge**.              |

正确说法：

- a piece of **advice**
- a piece of **information**
- a piece of **furniture**
- a piece of **equipment**
- a piece of **news**

例如：

✔ He gave me **a piece of advice**.

不是：

❌ an advice

例如：

**There is plenty of ______ available.**

答案：

✔ **parking**
